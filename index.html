Contributing

You are welcome to continue to develop this simulator and add different methods to improve obstacle avoidance. Things that would be cool to add:

  • automatic scene interpretation
  • anchor
  • object recognition
  • egometry from motion

Authors and Contributors

In the spring of 2016, Lars Brusletto (@larssbr) wrote his master thesis: Computer Vision based Obstacle Avoidance for an ROV. This is a bi-product of that work. He needed a simulator to test the feasibility of different obstacle avoidance algorithms.

Support or Contact

Having trouble with Aurlabcvsimulator? Check out the documentation at http://aurlabcvsimulator.readthedocs.io/ or contact support at (https://github.com/larssbr) or larsbrusletto@gmail.com and I will help you sort it out.
